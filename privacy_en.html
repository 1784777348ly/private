Introduction

Welcome to "Sports Performance" app. We understand the importance of personal information and are committed to protecting your privacy security. This guide explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Permission Usage

Bluetooth Permission

Used to connect sports sensors, heart rate straps and other devices to collect exercise data. We do not scan or connect to other unrelated devices.

Photo Library Permission

Used to save and read sports analysis screenshots, training records, etc.

Camera Permission

Used to record sports videos for motion analysis and posture correction.

Local File Permission

Used to store exercise data reports, training plans and other files.

Network Permission

Used to sync your exercise data to the cloud and get the latest training content.

Information We Collect

  • Device information: device model, operating system version
  • Exercise data: heart rate, duration, calories burned
  • Media files: sports photos, videos (only when authorized by you)
  • Personal data: height, weight, age (for personalized calculations)
  • Usage data: feature usage frequency, training duration

How We Use Information

  • Provide personalized sports analysis reports
  • Optimize app performance and stability
  • Generate sports reports and progress trends
  • Provide customer support services

Data Storage and Security

  • All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption
  • Strictly restrict personal information access permissions
  • Retain only for the period required for the purpose of use

Your Rights

  • Access rights: Query your personal information
  • Correction rights: Modify inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion rights: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdrawal of consent rights: Withdraw authorized information collection at any time
